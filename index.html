Artificial Momentum

is a project exploring how humans and intelligent systems learn to co-regulate, co-create, and evolve together. We start with the nervous system — because no amount of optimisation or automation matters if humans are dysregulated, overwhelmed, or burnt out. And we look toward the future — because AI isn’t replacing us, it’s reshaping the way we relate, think, and build. This is a home for deep thinkers, neurodivergent minds, cycle-aware women, parents, creators, and anyone trying to stay human in a world that’s accelerating. Here, we study what helps people and AI regulate, imagine, and evolve alongside each other — with clarity, compassion, and a future-oriented lens.
